Cycle-Gram

Cycle-Gram is a web application where users can create accounts and explore activities shared by other users. Users can add their bikes to their accounts, allowing others to view them for inspiration. In addition to browsing bikes, users can create and share routes they have ridden. The app also enables the creation of cycling events for users to join and ride together. As users scroll through the app, they receive recommendations for participating in cycling events or solo rides.

Features

  • Account creation and login
  • Browsing and adding bikes
  • Creating and sharing routes
  • Organizing and joining cycling events
  • Personalized recommendations

Bike Manufacturer Information

Each bike is associated with a manufacturer. Users can add manufacturers to their bikes by choosing from existing options. Manufacturer details are then displayed alongside bike information.

Project Goal

The goal of Cycle-Gram is to connect cycling enthusiasts, allowing them to share their passion for bikes and cycling adventures.
